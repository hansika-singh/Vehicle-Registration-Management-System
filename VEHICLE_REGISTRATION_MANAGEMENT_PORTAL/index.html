❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How long does new vehicle registration take? ▼
Online applications are typically processed within 5–7 working days. Temporary Registration (TR) is issued instantly upon submission. Permanent RC is dispatched by Speed Post within 15 working days after RTO approval.
What documents are needed for new registration? ▼
You need: (1) Form 20 (Registration Application), (2) Invoice from dealer, (3) Insurance certificate, (4) PUC certificate, (5) Aadhaar Card (address & identity proof), (6) PAN Card, (7) Passport-size photographs. Financed vehicles also need Form 34 & HP endorsement letter.
Can I track my RC delivery after approval? ▼
Yes! Once your RC is dispatched via Speed Post, you will receive an SMS with the tracking number. You can also check the status in the Track Application section of this portal under "RC Dispatch" status.
What happens if my RC expires? ▼
Driving with an expired RC is punishable under Section 192 of the Motor Vehicles Act with a fine of ₹5,000 or imprisonment up to 3 months. You can apply for renewal up to 60 days before expiry online. Late renewal attracts an additional penalty fee.
How do I pay road tax online? ▼
Go to Road Tax section → Enter your vehicle registration number → Select tax period → Choose payment method (UPI/Net Banking/Card) → Complete payment. You will receive an e-receipt via SMS and email within 24 hours.
What is NOC and when is it required? ▼
NOC (No Objection Certificate) is required when you permanently shift your vehicle to another state. It must be obtained from the issuing RTO before re-registering in the new state. NOC is valid for 6 months from date of issue.
